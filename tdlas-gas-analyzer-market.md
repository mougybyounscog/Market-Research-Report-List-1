<p><strong>The TDLAS Gas Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In Situ Laser Analyzer</li><li>Extractive Laser Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TDLAS (Tunable Diode Laser Absorption Spectroscopy) gas analyzer market is divided into two primary types: in situ laser analyzers and extractive laser analyzers. In situ laser analyzers measure gas concentrations directly within the process environment, offering real-time monitoring and reduced sampling errors. Conversely, extractive laser analyzers extract gas samples for analysis, providing higher sensitivity and accuracy in laboratory settings. Both types utilize the principles of laser absorption spectroscopy to detect specific gas species effectively.</p></p>
